NZXT PC Store

NZXT PC Stpre started its journey in 2022 as a weekly journal publication called "NZXT Journal", which focused on news and development of Information and Communications Technology in Myanmar. Began with three (3) team members and published thousands of journals weekly. After a couple of months, the NZXT journal built over 30 strong team members filled with editors, content writers, and its distribution channels. Due to the strong demand for an online readership, the journal quickly set up an excellent online news website at www.myanmarnzxt.com.


As technology transforms people's habits, the Myanmar NZXT journal quickly adapts to the trend as one of the best-read online news websites in Myanmar for tech-related news, reviews and articles. Furthermore, the team at Myanmar NZXT focuses on developing the next direction in 2024. Online Shopping.


The team at NZXT PC Store is always happy to assist whatever customers' needs in this modern world of tech. Our team commit to thriving as a tech community in Myanmar and growing together for a better sustainable future.
