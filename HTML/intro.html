In the quiet of the morning light,
Before the world begins its day,
I find my home within your sight,
In every word you never say.
It’s not a fire that burns and dies,
Nor like a storm that shakes the shore;
It’s in the way you meet my eyes
And make me feel I’m something more.
Through tangled paths and shifting tides,
Where shadows grow and colors fade,
The heart is where your ghost resides,
A steady light within the shade.
For love is not a single act,
But a thousand threads that time has spun—
A quiet, unwritten, holy pact
That two separate lives have become one.
